Certification

We help to open new horizons by opening the world of endless opportunities to our clients. Halal certification is a key to new markets and significant expansion of exports.

Certification Process

Our organization provides a service of Halal products’ certification. This certificate allows companies to export their products to Muslim countries. In addition, the certificate gives an opportunity for the companies to increase the sales of their products in other countries and markets with a need for Halal products.

World Halal Trust Group provides certification according to 6 standards, depending on the countries of export.

Gulf Countries

(GSO 2055-1:2015, GSO 993/2015)

Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia

(MS 1500:2019)

Europe, Asia, CIS countries, America, Africa

(WHT.G:2022)

Turkey, Pakistan, Middle Asia

(OIC/SMIIC 1:2019)

Catering, Halal food and tourism services

(OIC/SMIIC 6:2019 or OIC/SMIIC 9:2019)
